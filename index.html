The Legal Practice Area

Business Law

Business law governs the rights, relations, and conduct of individuals and companies in commercial matters.

Criminal Law

Criminal law addresses behavior that is considered harmful to society and provides punishment for those who break the law.

Child Support

Child support law ensures financial assistance for children from both parents after a separation or divorce.

Education Law

Education law protects the rights of students, teachers, and institutions, ensuring fair and equal access to education.

Divorce Law

Divorce law manages the legal process of ending a marriage, including child custody, asset division, and support.

Tax Law

Tax law focuses on how individuals and businesses are taxed, helping to ensure compliance with financial regulations.
